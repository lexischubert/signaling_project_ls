Welcome to the Image Labelling Study

Thank you for taking part in this study.

In this study you will view a series of images, one at a time, and answer a question about each image in your own words.

The study is divided into two sessions of 90 minutes each, with an optional 15-minute break in between.

Before you begin, please note:

  • Ensure you are in a quiet environment where you will not be interrupted.
  • Do not close or refresh the browser window during the task.
  • Work at a steady pace — there is no minimum time required per image.
  • Your answers will be recorded automatically.

Total estimated time: approximately 3 hours and 15 minutes (including break).
