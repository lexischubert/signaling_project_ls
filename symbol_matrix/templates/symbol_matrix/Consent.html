Madhumuni ya utafiti

Tunakukaribisha kushiriki katika utafiti wa maamuzi na kazi za akili. Utafiti huu unafanywa na watafiti kutoka Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Chuo Kikuu cha California Berkeley, na Chuo Kikuu cha Zurich. Utafiti huu unahusisha kujibu haraka na kwa usahihi picha zinazoonekana kwenye skrini yako.

Utafanya nini

{% if condition == 'no_break' %} Utakamilisha kazi ya kulinganisha alama: kipindi kina muda wa dakika 90, bila mapumziko. {% elif condition == 'forced_break' %} Utakamilisha kazi ya kulinganisha alama katika vipindi viwili vya dakika {{ task_minutes }} kila kimoja, vikigawanywa na mapumziko ya lazima ya dakika {{ break_minutes }}. {% else %} Utakamilisha kazi ya kulinganisha alama katika vipindi viwili vya dakika {{ task_minutes }} kila kimoja, na mapumziko ya hiari ya dakika {{ break_minutes }} kati ya vipindi. {% endif %} Katika raundi moja, utaona alama lengwa na lazima uchague alama zote zinazolingana kutoka kwenye gridi ya alama 100.

Malipo

Utapokea malipo ya ushiriki ya Ksh 350 kwa kukamilisha utafiti wote. Pia tunafunika nauli yako ya Ksh 200. Zaidi ya hayo, unaweza kupata malipo ya ziada ya bonasi kulingana na usahihi na kasi yako ya kazi. Kwa jumla, unaweza kutarajia kupata kati ya Ksh 600–700. Mapato yako yote yatalipwa kupitia M-PESA. Tutahesabu mapato mara moja, lakini inaweza kuchukua siku moja au mbili kupokea malipo.

Hatari na manufaa

Hakuna hatari zinazojulikana zaidi ya hatari za kawaida za matumizi ya kompyuta. Hakuna faida ya moja kwa moja ya kibinafsi zaidi ya malipo yaliyoelezwa hapo juu, lakini majibu yako yatachangia utafiti wa kisayansi.

Usiri

Taarifa zinazotambulisha zitahifadhiwa kwa siri na hazitashirikiwa nje ya timu ya utafiti. Data zote huhifadhiwa kwa usalama na zitaripotiwa tu kwa njia isiyotambulisha watu binafsi. Jina lako halitahusishwa na majibu yako katika chapisho lolote.

Ushiriki wa hiari

Ushiriki ni wa hiari kabisa, lakini lazima uwe na umri wa zaidi ya miaka 18. Unaweza kuacha wakati wowote bila adhabu, ingawa utapokea malipo kamili tu ukikamilisha utafiti. Ikiwa una maswali, tafadhali uliza timu ya utafiti kabla ya kuendelea.

Purpose of the study

We invite you to take part in a research study on decision-making and cognitive tasks. The study is conducted by researchers from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, the University of California Berkeley, the University of Zurich. The study involves responding quickly and correctly to images on your screen.

What you will do

{% if condition == 'no_break' %} You will complete a symbol-matching task: the session lasts 90 minutes, with no break. {% elif condition == 'forced_break' %} You will complete a symbol-matching task in two sessions of {{ task_minutes }} minutes each, separated by a required {{ break_minutes }}-minute break. {% else %} You will complete a symbol-matching task in two sessions of {{ task_minutes }} minutes each, with an optional {{ break_minutes }}-minute break in between. {% endif %} During a given round, you will see a target symbol and must select all matching symbols from a grid of 100.

Payment

You will receive a participation payment of Ksh 350 for completing the full study. We also cover your transport for Ksh 200. In addition, you could earn additional bonus payments based on task accuracy and speed. In total, you can expect to earn around Ksh 600–700. Your total earnings will be paid via MPesa. We will calculate earnings immediately, but it may take a day or two for the payments to arrive.

Risks and benefits

There are no known risks beyond those of everyday computer use. There is no direct personal benefit beyond the payment described above, but your responses will contribute to scientific research.

Confidentiality

Identifying information will be kept strictly confidential and will not be shared outside the research team. All data are stored securely and will only be reported in anonymized, aggregated form. Your name will never be linked to your responses in any publication.

Voluntary participation

Participation is entirely voluntary, but you must be over the age of 18. You may stop at any time without penalty, though you will only receive the full payment if you complete the study. If you have questions, please ask the research team before proceeding.
