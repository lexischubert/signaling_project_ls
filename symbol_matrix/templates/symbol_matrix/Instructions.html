How the task works

  1. You will be shown one large image at a time, together with a question about that image.
  2. Type your answer in the text box below the image.
  3. Click Submit Answer (or press Ctrl + Enter) to move to the next image.
  4. The next image loads immediately after you submit — there is no break between images unless you choose one.
  5. A countdown timer in the top-right corner shows how much time remains in the current session.
  6. When the first 40-minute session ends you will be asked whether you want a 10-minute break.
    • If you take the break, the screen will be locked for 10 minutes and then the second session will start automatically.
    • If you skip the break, you will continue labelling images immediately; your second session of 40 minutes will begin straight after.
A short practice trial follows on the next page so you can familiarise yourself with the layout before the timed sessions begin.
